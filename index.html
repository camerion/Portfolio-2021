About Me

My name is Camerion Brooks. I got into tech because I am a problem solver. I love taking complex issues, breaking them down, and seeing the results of my work. Before making the switch to web development, I worked in customer service, where I enjoyed resolving customer issues by working diligently to deliver desired results or point them in the right direction. The end results from customer service are linked to the same joy I feel when working as a developer.

Development makes me happy! I can fix, build, and see my results in real-time. I am most excited when starting a new project. I like to experiment with code and see how I can continually improve. Coding for me is the active process of problem-solving. It is how I get to do what I love all the time.
